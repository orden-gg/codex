Follow these steps to get up and running with Fireball.

## Step 1: Create a Gigaverse Account

If you don't have one yet, create an account at [gigaverse.io](https://gigaverse.io). This sets up your Abstract wallet that you'll use across both Gigaverse and Fireball.

## Step 2: Sign In to Fireball

Head to [Fireball](https://fireball.gg) and sign in using the same Abstract wallet as your Gigaverse account. You'll be asked to sign a message — this is the same message Gigaverse uses and allows Fireball to make in-game moves on your behalf.

<Callout type="info">
  The message you sign does **NOT** allow Fireball to make any transactions with your wallet. Your
  funds **CANNOT** be accessed. Fireball can only perform in-game actions like dungeon moves,
  fishing plays, and loot collection.
</Callout>

If you were referred by another player, make sure to use their referral link when signing up. You can also sign in via Discord if your Discord account is linked to your Gigaverse profile.

## Step 3: Claim Your Free Trial

Once signed in, head to the **Quests** page from the sidebar. You'll find the **Scroll of Noob** quest — claim it to get a **5-day free trial** of Fireball's game features (dungeons and fishing).

<Callout type="info">
  The Scroll of Noob covers dungeon and fishing runs. Automated [Services](/guides/services) (like
  Fish Seller and ROM Claimer) require Sparks even during the trial, unless you have a **Scroll of
  Fire**.
</Callout>

## Step 4: Get Giga-Juiced

To access Fireball's full features after your trial, you need to be under the effects of **Giga-Juice** in Gigaverse. Make sure your Giga-Juice is active — you can check its status and expiration in the Fireball dashboard sidebar.

## Step 5: Purchase Sparks

After your trial ends, you'll need [Sparks](/guides/sparks) to keep using Fireball. Head to the **Sparks** page to purchase them with ETH.

The Sparks page includes a **cost calculator** — select your game type (Dungeon or Fishing) and a time range (day, week, or month) to estimate how many Sparks you'll need based on your level and energy.

[Learn more about Sparks](/guides/sparks)

## Your First Run

Now that you're set up, here's how to start your first automated game:

### Starting a Dungeon Run

1. Go to **Play** in the sidebar
2. Select **Dungeon**
3. Choose your dungeon type (start with **Normal** if you're new)
4. Pick an agent AI preset — **Balanced** is a good default
5. Set the number of runs you want
6. Review the Spark cost and hit **Start**

### Starting a Fishing Session

1. Go to **Play** in the sidebar
2. Select **Fishing**
3. Choose a fishing type — **Normal** is a solid starting point
4. Choose **Agent** mode for automated play, or **Manual** if you want to play with hints
5. Set the number of sessions and hit **Start**

Once started, your runs appear in the **Jobs** page. You can monitor progress there or simply close your browser — Fireball runs everything in the background.

## Navigating the Dashboard

After signing in, you'll land on the dashboard. Here's a quick overview of what's in the sidebar:

| Section       | What It Does                                                                |
| ------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Play**      | Start dungeon runs, fishing sessions, or enter Conquest                     |
| **Jobs**      | View and manage your active and past automation jobs                        |
| **Inventory** | Browse your items, filter by type, and see total value                      |
| **Pets**      | Manage pets and eggs — feed, hatch, and filter                              |
| **World**     | Access crafting: Alchemy, Workbench, Merchant, Cooking, Hatchery, and Stubs |
| **Services**  | Set up automated Fish Seller, ROM Claimer, Loot Collector, and Hatchery     |
| **Dungeons**  | View your dungeon run history with filters and win rates                    |
| **Fishing**   | View your fishing history with filters and catch rates                      |
| **ROMs**      | Manage your ROMs                                                            |
| **Quests**    | Complete quests and claim rewards including scrolls                         |
| **Referrals** | Get your referral link and track earnings                                   |

## Installing the App

Fireball is available as a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**. You can install it directly from your browser for a native app-like experience with push notifications.

- On desktop: Look for the install icon in your browser's address bar
- On mobile: Use your browser's "Add to Home Screen" option
- Complete the **PWA Install** quest to earn bonus Sparks

## Background Operation

One of Fireball's best features is that **you don't need to keep the browser tab open**. Once you start your runs, you can safely:

- Close your browser tab
- Turn off your PC
- Go about your day

Fireball handles all your scheduled runs on its servers. Come back anytime to check your results.
