Quests are tasks you can complete to earn rewards like Scrolls, Sparks, and XP. Find them on the **Quests** page in the dashboard sidebar.

## How Quests Work

Quests are displayed as cards sorted by priority. Each quest shows:

- Quest image and name
- Description of what to do
- Rewards you'll earn
- Requirements to claim (if any)
- Current status (active, completed, inactive, or expired)

When you've met a quest's requirements, click **Claim Quest** to collect your rewards.

## Reward Types

| Type        | Description                                 |
| ----------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| **Sparks**  | Credits added to your Spark balance         |
| **Scrolls** | Special items that grant access to features |
| **XP**      | Experience points for your Fireball profile |

## Key Quests

### Scroll of Noob

Your first quest — claim it to get a **5-day free trial** of Fireball's game features (dungeons and fishing). This is the best way to try Fireball before purchasing Sparks.

<Callout type="info">
  The Scroll of Noob covers game runs only. [Services](/guides/services) still require Sparks unless
  you have the Scroll of Fire.
</Callout>

### Scroll of Fire

A permanent scroll that grants **free access to games and all Services** — dungeon/fishing runs plus Fish Seller, ROM Claimer, Loot Collector, and Hatchery operate without consuming Sparks while you hold this scroll.

### PWA Install Quest

Install Fireball as a **Progressive Web App** on your mobile device and earn bonus Sparks. This quest can only be claimed from the installed mobile app.

## Quest Status

| Status        | Meaning                                            |
| ------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **Active**    | Available to claim when requirements are met       |
| **Completed** | Already claimed — shows "Reward Claimed"           |
| **Expired**   | Past the expiration date, can no longer be claimed |
| **Inactive**  | Not currently available                            |

## Tips

- **Claim the Scroll of Noob first** — it's your free trial and the best starting point
- **Install the mobile app** for easy Spark rewards from the PWA quest
- **Check quests regularly** — new quests may appear over time
