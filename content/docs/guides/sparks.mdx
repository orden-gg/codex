Sparks are Fireball's credit system — the fuel that powers automated dungeon runs, fishing sessions, and background services. Every action Fireball takes on your behalf consumes Sparks.

## What Are Sparks?

Sparks are credits within the Fireball platform. They are **not** a cryptocurrency token and never will be. Think of them as the operating cost for Fireball's automation features.

You need Sparks to:

- Run automated dungeon battles
- Run automated fishing sessions
- Power background services (Fish Seller, ROM Claimer, Loot Collector, Hatchery)
- Use fishing hints in manual mode
- Earn Fireball XP

## How to Get Sparks

### Free Trial — Scroll of Noob

New users can claim the **Scroll of Noob** from the [Quests](/guides/quests-and-rewards) page for a **5-day free trial** of dungeon and fishing runs.

<Callout type="info">
  The Scroll of Noob covers game runs only. [Services](/guides/services) still require Sparks unless
  you have the **Scroll of Fire**, which grants free access to game runs and all services.
</Callout>

### Purchasing with ETH

After your trial, purchase Sparks on the [Sparks page](/guides/buying-sparks) using ETH. The page includes a cost calculator to estimate your needs based on game type and time range.

### Quests & Airdrops

Some [quests](/guides/quests-and-rewards) reward Sparks — like the PWA Install quest. Occasional airdrops may also grant free Sparks.

## Spark Costs

Different activities consume different amounts of Sparks. Costs vary by game type, dungeon/fishing subtype, and agent AI model. Your account skills can also reduce costs.

| Activity        | Cost Basis                         |
| --------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Dungeon Run     | Varies by dungeon type and model   |
| Fishing Session | Varies by fishing type             |
| Services        | Per invocation (varies by service) |
| Fishing Hints   | Per hint used in manual mode       |

The exact Spark cost is always shown before you start a run or subscribe to a service. See [Spark Costs](/reference/spark-costs) for more details.

## Managing Your Sparks

### Viewing Your Balance

Your Spark balance is displayed in the dashboard. The Sparks page shows additional details including your 30-day purchase limit.

### Transaction History

Track every Spark movement on the [Transactions](/features/dashboard) page. Each transaction shows:

- **Date** — When it occurred
- **Type** — Game, Purchase, Airdrop, Service, or Hint
- **Amount** — Sparks consumed or added (with +/- indicator)
- **Running balance** — Your balance after that transaction
- **Details** — Links to the specific dungeon, fishing session, or service

Filter by transaction type to focus on specific activity.

### 30-Day Period Limit

Each account can purchase a maximum of **20,000 Sparks per 30-day rolling period**. This limit is enforced by the smart contract. The Sparks page shows your remaining allowance.

## Requirements

To use Sparks, you must:

1. Have an active **Gigaverse account**
2. Be under the effects of **Giga-Juice**
3. Have a positive **Spark balance** (or an active scroll)

## Tips

- **Use the cost calculator** before purchasing — select your game type and time range to estimate needs
- **Monitor your transactions** to understand consumption patterns
- **Start with fewer runs** to learn the costs before buying in bulk
- **The Scroll of Fire** is your best value overall — it removes Spark costs for both game runs and services
