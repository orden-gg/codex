Invite other players to Fireball and earn a percentage of what they spend. Find your referral link and track earnings on the **Referrals** page.

## How It Works

1. **Copy your referral link** from the Referrals page
2. **Share it** with other players
3. When they sign up using your link, they become your referral
4. You earn **10% of their premium service spending** as ETH

Your referral link format: `https://fireball.gg/login?ref={yourAccountName}`

## Tracking Earnings

The Referrals page shows:

- **Total referrals** — How many players signed up with your link
- **Total earnings** — Your accumulated earnings in ETH (with USD conversion)
- **Referral table** — List of all referrals with join date, name, faction, and approval status

### Referral Statuses

| Status       | Meaning                                  |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------- |
| **Approved** | Active referral, earning you commissions |
| **Pending**  | Account awaiting approval                |

The table is paginated (15 per page) and sorted by join date.

## Tips

- Share your link in communities where players are interested in Gigaverse
- The referral is tracked via the `?ref=` parameter — make sure your link is complete
