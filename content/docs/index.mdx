Fireball is **the** go-to complementary game client, parallel meta-game and autobattler for [Gigaverse](https://gigaverse.io). It helps you save precious time, focus on strategy, and engage in this parallel ecosystem.

## What You Can Do

- **Automate dungeon runs** — Choose your agent AI model and let Fireball handle combat across Normal and Underhaul dungeons
- **Manage fishing** — Run automated fishing sessions or play manually with hints
- **Automate services** — Set up Fish Seller, ROM Claimer, Loot Collector, and Hatchery to run on autopilot
- **Craft and trade** — Access Alchemy, Workbench, Merchant, Cooking, and more through the World
- **Manage pets and ROMs** — Track your eggs, feed pets, and claim ROM rewards
- **Track everything** — View stats, loot, game history, transactions, and performance metrics
- **Play in the background** — Close your browser and let Fireball work while you're away

## How It Works

Fireball runs **in the background** — you don't need to keep your browser open. Start your runs, close the tab, and come back later to collect your rewards. Your jobs continue running on Fireball's servers until completion.

1. **Sign in** with your Abstract wallet (the same one you use on Gigaverse)
2. **Configure** your game — pick a dungeon type, fishing size, or set up a service
3. **Start** your runs and walk away
4. **Come back** to check results, collect loot, and review your history
