Fishing is one of the core game modes in Gigaverse. Each fishing session pits you against a fish in a card-based battle — play the right cards to deal damage, weaken the fish, and reel it in. Fireball can automate the entire process or give you hints while you play manually.

## How It Works

1. **Choose a fishing type** — Small, Normal, or Big
2. **Pick a mode** — Agent (automated) or Manual (play with agent AI hints)
3. **Set the number of sessions** — Based on your energy and daily limits
4. **Start fishing** — Fireball handles the rest, or you play card by card

Once started, your sessions appear in the [Jobs](/features/jobs) page. You can watch live via the fishing viewer, or close your browser and come back later.

## Fishing Types

| Type       | Energy Cost | Daily Limit | Juiced Limit | Description                       |
| ---------- | ----------- | ----------- | ------------ | --------------------------------- |
| **Small**  | 12          | 10          | 20           | Lower difficulty, smaller rewards |
| **Normal** | 16          | 10          | 20           | Balanced difficulty and rewards   |
| **Big**    | 20          | 10          | 20           | Toughest fish, best rewards       |

<Callout type="info">
  **Juiced mode** doubles your daily limit from 10 to 20 runs for all fishing types. Values are
  subject to change based on game configuration.
</Callout>

## Gameplay Modes

### Agent Mode (Automated)

In Agent mode, Fireball's agent AI plays the entire session for you. The agent AI:

- Evaluates each card's potential outcome
- Calculates redraw probabilities
- Selects the highest expected return play
- Handles multiple consecutive sessions automatically

Agent mode supports all configuration options including autoclaim, autorepair, and equipment selection.

### Manual Mode

In Manual mode, you play the fishing game yourself with optional **agent AI hints**. You're limited to one session at a time but get full control over every card decision.

Manual mode is great for learning the fishing mechanics, having fun with the card game, or when you want a more hands-on experience.

#### Hint System

When playing manually, Fireball can show you agent AI-powered hints for each decision:

- **Recommended action** — "Play \{card\}", "Redraw your hand", or "Choose \{card\}"
- **Reasoning** — Why the agent AI recommends this move
- **Confidence level** — Color-coded from Very High (90%+) down to Very Low (&lt;30%)

A tutorial modal explains the hint system the first time you use it. Free hints are included daily, with additional hints available via Sparks.

## Configuration Options

Before starting a fishing session, you can configure:

| Option             | Description                                                                                                                                          |
| ------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Runs**           | Capped by energy, daily limits, and sparks                                                                                                           |
| **Autoclaim**      | Automatically claim energy from ROMs if needed                                                                                                       |
| **Autorepair**     | Auto-repair broken gear during the session. If gear is beyond repair, Fireball will swap it for another piece of the same rarity from your inventory |
| **Jebaitor Skill** | Use Jebaitor skill when at daily limit (if you have the skill)                                                                                       |
| **Equipment**      | Select 1 Rod and up to 2 Lures for the session                                                                                                       |

A **Repeat Previous Run** button appears after your first session, letting you quickly reuse your last configuration.

## Live Fishing Viewer

While a session is in progress, you can watch it live. The viewer shows:

- **Fish health bar** — Track damage dealt to the fish
- **Your hand** — See your current cards and select plays (manual mode)
- **Redraw option** — Reshuffle your hand without playing
- **Round counter** — Track the current round
- **Damage numbers** — Floating indicators for hits, misses, redraws, and catches
- **Encounter history** — Full log of every action taken
- **Fishing drops** — Items and rewards earned

On desktop, the sidebar shows your account level, equipped gear, encounter history, and drops. On mobile, these are available as collapsible sheets.

## Fishing History

View your past sessions on the **Fishing** page (sidebar → Games → Fishing). The history page includes:

- **Type filter** — show All, Small, Normal, or Big sessions
- **Caught-only filter** — toggle to show only successful catches
- **Result limit** — display 20, 50, or 100 sessions
- **Catch rate** — see your overall catch rate for the filtered results

## Tips

- **Use Agent mode** for efficient grinding — the agent AI makes optimal decisions
- **Try Manual mode** to learn — the hint system teaches you the card strategy
- **Equip your best rod and lures** — gear significantly impacts your catch rate
- **Check your equipment durability** before queuing multiple sessions
