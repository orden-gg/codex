The World page is Fireball's crafting hub — six categories of recipes for brewing potions, forging gear, cooking food, hatching eggs, and more. Each category has its own mechanics, timers, and requirements.

## Crafting Categories

| Category      | Tag           | What It Covers                                 |
| ------------- | ------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **Alchemy**   | Crafting      | Potions, elixirs, and base crafting recipes    |
| **Stubs**     | Deals         | Daily and weekly deals with special pricing    |
| **Merchant**  | Deals-Buyback | Buyback options for previously sold items      |
| **Workbench** | Workbench     | Gear, charms, and tools                        |
| **Hatchery**  | Vilhelm       | Egg trades and weekly hatching recipes         |
| **Cooking**   | Cooking       | Food recipes generated from your fishing state |

## Category Details

### Alchemy

Base crafting recipes for potions and materials. Browse all available recipes or search by name.

### Stubs (Deals)

Special deals that refresh on a timer:

- **Daily deals** — Reset every 24 hours
- **Weekly deals** — Reset every 7 days

A countdown timer at the top shows when the next reset occurs.

### Merchant (Buyback)

Buyback options to re-acquire previously sold items at set prices.

### Workbench

Forge equipment with three sub-categories:

- **Gear** — Armor and weapons
- **Charm** — Stat-boosting charms
- **Tools** — Rods, lures, and utility items

### Hatchery (Vilhelm)

Egg-related recipes with two sub-categories:

- **Trades** — Always available, no time limit
- **Weekly** — 7-day rotation recipes

### Cooking

Food recipes generated from your fishing state. Cook your catches into useful items.

## Recipe Browser

Each category shows a list of available recipes. For every recipe, you can see:

- **Recipe name and icon**
- **Materials required** — with your current inventory count
- **Energy cost** — if applicable
- **Faction requirements** — some recipes are faction-specific
- **Completion limits** — daily, weekly, or maximum crafts

### Search

A search bar at the top filters recipes by name across the selected category.

### "Can Craft" Filter

Toggle this filter to show only recipes where you have enough materials to craft right now. This is the fastest way to find what's available to you.

## Crafting a Recipe

Click any recipe to open the **recipe drawer**, which shows:

- Full material list with your inventory quantities
- Whether you have enough of each material
- Energy requirements
- The craft button (disabled if you're missing materials or energy)

## Timers

Daily and weekly resets are tracked with countdown timers at the top of applicable categories. When a timer expires, new recipes and deals become available.
