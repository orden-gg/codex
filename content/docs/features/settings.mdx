The Settings page lets you manage your account details, notification preferences, audio levels, and Discord connection. Access it from the dashboard sidebar.

## Account Settings

### Abstract Wallet

View your connected wallet details:

- **Wallet address** — Displayed in shortened format (first 6 + last 4 characters) with a copy button
- **ETH balance** — Current balance with 4 decimal places and USD equivalent

### Discord Integration

Link your Discord account to Fireball for additional features:

- **Link Discord** — Connect via Discord OAuth
- **Unlink Discord** — Remove the connection
- **Status badge** — Shows "Active Session", "Limited Access", or "Not Linked"
- **Discord avatar and username** — Displayed when linked, with link date

## Profile Picture

Upload or update your profile picture displayed across the Fireball platform.

## Volume Settings

Control audio levels with three independent sliders:

| Slider                   | Range  | Description                   |
| ------------------------ | ------ | ----------------------------- |
| **Master Volume**        | 0–100% | Overall volume control        |
| **Music Volume**         | 0–100% | Background music level        |
| **Sound Effects Volume** | 0–100% | UI and gameplay sound effects |

A **mute toggle** button silences all audio at once.

## Notification Settings

Configure push notifications for your Fireball account. See [Notifications](/features/notifications) for full details.

### Quick Overview

- **Enable/Disable all** — Master toggle for push notifications
- **Per-type toggles** — Enable or disable specific notification types
- **Browser permission** — Fireball will prompt for notification permission if needed

<Callout type="info">
  Push notifications require browser notification permissions. If you've blocked notifications in
  your browser, you'll see a warning with instructions to re-enable them.
</Callout>
